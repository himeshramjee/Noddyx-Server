    Born out of my lack of knowledge around landscaping and maintaining a garden. This is a portal and app for neighbourhoods to sign up and drive community wide gardening. This will include integration with smart devices that for example can help track rainfall, level of sunshine. These would feed into the portal and individual garden profiles.
